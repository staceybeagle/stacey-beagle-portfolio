Introduction

I am a former college instructor and current stay-at-home mother to two amazing girls, aged 7 and 9. Now that my daughters are growing up, I'm ready to start my second career in Front-End Development.

I first became interested in web development when I started my own blog. I used WordPress as a starting point for my website, but soon became frustrated with my inability to personalize it exactly how I wanted to. So I began to teach myself HTML, CSS, PHP and Java. I was soon completely hooked and now spend my time coding while my kids are at school!

Where I'm From

I'm a California girl! I was raised in Petaluma, California where I grew up with small-town suburban values in one of the most beautiful places in the state. I have since settled in Roseville, California where I have the ocean two hours to the west, and the mountains two hours east.

Roseville is a town located just east of Sacramento. It's a perfect place to raise my girls because I can still offer them the same small-town values I grew up with but also expose them to the benefits of city living.

Because I was born and bred as a California girl, California is most likely where I am going to stay. However, once I reach my golden years I'm heading a bit farther east to live in the mountains!
